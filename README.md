## Overview
Supplychain360 is a fast-growing retail distribution company that manages distribution for hundreds of retail stores. Over the past year, the company has been expeiencing serious operational inefficiencies in it's supply chain, the challenges they're currently facing include **data fragmentation**, as operational data is spread across multiple systems and Stale data making it diffiult to answer business questions accurately.
